About Account
If you have a problem with your account balance:  You should communicate with the administrator in the nearest library as soon as possible.

If your account is illegally logged in:  You should change your password immediately and get in touch with our Cyber Security Center.

If you are about to graduate, there are still many account balances:  We will send you a check within a week of leaving school.

If you have insufficient account balance before graduation:   Please pay off the balance even before graduation.
